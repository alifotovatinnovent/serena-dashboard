❓ How Serena Works

When You Submit a Proposal, Here's What Happens:

📝 Step 1: You Submit

Fill out the proposal form with client name, contacts, deal value, and key value propositions. Click "Launch Campaign" and Serena takes over.

🔍 Step 2: Research (Apollo.io)

What Serena Does: Enriches your contact data, finds additional stakeholders, maps the org chart, identifies decision-makers by role (CFO, CTO, Ops).

You See: "Found 5 stakeholders via Apollo • Mapped org structure"

🎯 Step 3: Plan

What Serena Does: Creates a multi-touch campaign strategy. Email sequence (Day 1 ROI, Day 3 Features, Day 7 Case Study, Day 14 Demo). LinkedIn connection + message sequence. Personalizes content by stakeholder role.

You See: "5-touch sequence created (Email + LinkedIn)"

📧 Step 4: Execute (Outlook + MeetAlfred)

What Serena Does: Sends personalized emails via your Outlook (amanda.f@innovent.io). LinkedIn connection requests via MeetAlfred. Customizes messaging per stakeholder (CFO gets ROI, CTO gets tech specs).

You See: "3 emails sent via Outlook • 2 LinkedIn connections via MeetAlfred"

📊 Step 5: Monitor & Adapt

What Serena Does: Tracks email opens, clicks, replies. Monitors LinkedIn connection accepts, message responses. Calculates engagement scores. Detects buying signals ("What's the pricing?" = hot lead). Adapts strategy in real-time (hot leads get accelerated, cold leads get pivoted).

You See: Real-time metrics + AI recommendations

🤝 Step 6: Close

What Serena Does: Alerts you when buying signals detected. Hands off hot leads for your personal follow-up. Continues nurturing warm/cold leads.

You See: 🔥 HOT badge + high-priority AI recommendation

🤖 AI Recommendations

Serena analyzes all engagement data and suggests specific next actions:

  • High Priority: Immediate actions for hot leads (send technical doc, schedule demo)
  • Medium Priority: Strategy pivots for cold leads (change messaging angle)
  • Low Priority: Expansion opportunities (add more stakeholders, try new channels)

📊 Results You Track

Every campaign shows real-time metrics from all tools:

  • Apollo.io: Contacts found, stakeholders mapped
  • Outlook: Emails sent, opens, clicks
  • MeetAlfred: LinkedIn connections, messages, responses
  • Sales Navigator: Additional prospects discovered
  • Serena AI: Overall engagement score, recommendations
